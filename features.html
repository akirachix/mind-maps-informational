Who is
CoopConnect?

CoopConnect is a platform aiming for empowering farmers through accessible, on-demand agricultural training.

KEY FEATURES

Authentication
Easy account creation and secure farmer verification for personalized experiences.
Scheduling
Farmers can view sessions and book training at their convenience, increasing participation.
Trainings
Access modules on key farming topics, delivered by experts, anytime, from any place.
Rewards
Earn points for attending trainings and redeem them for farming inputs or support.
