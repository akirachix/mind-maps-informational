How It Works

We make cooperative training for farmers easy and impactful. Discover our step-by-step process
that brings together farmers, extension workers, and technology to boost productivity and cooperation.

Our Process

CoopConnect guides farmers and cooperatives through these key steps for better results and sustainable growth.

👥

Grouping Farmers

Farmers are grouped by village, forming collaborative learning circles for support and sharing.

🧑‍🏫

Assigning Extensions

Extension workers are assigned to each group, offering expert guidance and practical training.

🗓️

Sessions

Sessions are scheduled regularly for each group, ensuring consistent, ongoing learning opportunities.

🏅

Farmer's Points

Active participation is rewarded with points, motivating farmers to attend and engage in every session.
